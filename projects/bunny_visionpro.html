Bunny-VisionPro is a robot teleoperation system integrated with Apple Vision Pro as pose tracking device, specifically tailored for bimanual dexterous manipulation. The system boasts a highly modular architecture, simplifying deployment and enhancing flexibility. In this project, we demonstrate that our robust teleoperation system can perform various kinds of tasks such as skincare, kitchen tasks, dynamic tasks, and motion-constrained tasks. Such resilient and versatile teleoperation system can be deployed to collect high-quality data for imitation learning. We showcase the learned autonomous policy in a bimanual dexterous setup across a range of household tasks.
