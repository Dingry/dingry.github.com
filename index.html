Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student (2020-) at EEE, The University of Hong Kong, advised by Dr. Xiaojuan Qi. Before that I obtained my B.Eng. degree in the School of Software Engineering at Tsinghua University in 2020.

My research interests focus on 3D vision with deep learning, especially 3D representation learning and 3D scene understanding.
